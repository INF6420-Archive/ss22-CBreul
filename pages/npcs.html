NPCs

King Varuuk

Human Beastmaster Barbarian

The Beast King

King Varuuk, known as the Beast King, is the founder of Nettep. Having an intense bond and ability to communicate with animals, he was able to bond with the dinosaurs that roam the rainforest, and introduced them as companions/working animals. He recently lost his long-time wife, and had recently taken up with Zihu, a Yuan-ti dancer.

Ront Liadon

Half Orc/Half Elf Fighter

Heir to the Liadon Rare Goods Empire

Ront Liadon is a handsome, charming - if not smarmy - business man who deals in the realm of rare magical goods. He can source anything for you - for a price.

Lupey Mergen

Halfling Artificer

Purveyor of All Things Odd

Lupey Mergen is an eccentric artificer-slash-chemist, who holes up in her potion shop with her trusty famiiar, a semi-sentient blimp. She has a deep knowledge of the rainforest's plants and shelves full of potions and books. She seems indifferent to the political unrest fomenting in Nettep, content to study in her own space.

Monsters and Enemies

Galta

Primal Tyrannosaurus

The Primal Hunger

A massive Tyrannosaurus covered in teeth, Galta is one of the three Primals stalking Nettep. Her hunger cannot be sated, despite constantly eating. She can compel her prey to approach her (charm), as well as influence creatures around her to become similarly bloodthirsty. From the plane of Pandemonium, she leaks planar corruption which can physically alter living beings around her, such as plants.

Elemental Locus of Caustic Waters

Water Elemental

Formed in the amber bones of a massive coelacanth, the Elemental Locus of Caustic Waters sat on top of a secondary cutting of Knot's home tree. Upon defeat, the cutting known as the Primal Wellspring was reawakened.

Defeated.

Elemental Locus of Coruscating Flame

Fire Elemental

The Elemental Locus of Coruscating Flame was an elemental that had coalesced from the iron bones of a Primal wolf which sat on top of a secondary cutting of Knot's home tree. Upon defeat, the cutting known as the Primal Forge was reawakened.

Defeated.

Elemental Locus of Battering Winds

Air Elemental

An air elemental formed on the bones of a Primal Pteradon.

Defeated.
