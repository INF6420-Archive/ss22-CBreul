Freja

Half-Orc Ranger/Rogue

The Huntress

Freja is a hardened ranger-rogue used to doing things alone - or rather, with just her faithful canine compaion, a wolf named Feng. Currently a member of a shadowy theiving organization, this one-time-lycanthrope has as many agendas as she has arrows in her quiver.

Likes
  • Shane, her husband
  • Her bestie, Feng
  • The blood of her enemies
Dislikes
  • Anyone who doesn't like Feng
  • Blowing her cover
  • Wasting perfectly good arrows

Signature Moves and Achievements

  • Using Anhur's Grasp to deliver a deadly final blow.
  • Once successfully mastered lycanthropy in Intepueh.
  • Two words: Suprise Attack.

Knot Anent

Arborin Moon Circle Druid

The Tree of Many Shapes

Knot is an Arborin, a child of a massive, magical magnolia tree. Slow to trust, and deeply concerned with the wellbeing of the plants and animals around him, Knot can sometimes be uncertain about his compatriots, though their bonds grow as surely as his leaves do.

Likes
  • Nature.
  • His best friend, Taddy Mason
  • Turning into a bird and flying away
Dislikes
  • Fire
  • Books, paper, or anything made of trees
  • Wasting perfectly good arrows

Signature Moves and Achievements

  • Summoning a herd of Satyrs to play a group of enemies - and sometimes friends - to sleep.
  • Befriending at any animal that comes across his path to acquire their Wildshape.
  • Making Goodberries at clutch moments to save his teammates.

Janaan

Water Genasi Monk

Faster Than You

Janaan, a happy-go-lucky monk, is thrilled with her new group of friends and the adventures they go on. While she seeks to find more about her mother, she enjoys the hearty bouts and magical mischief the party gets into. Indescribably good at hand-to-hand combat and as pretty as a peach, Janaan is not one to be underestimated.

Likes
  • Water! Lakes, rivers, ponds, puddles
  • A good beat and a handsome dance partner
  • Throwing hands with whoever is mean to her or her friends
Dislikes
  • Rude people
  • Deserts and dry places
  • Bad music

Signature Moves and Achievements

  • Can move so far and so fast that she can lap the entire party in one turn.
  • Took out a Lamia by parkour-ing off a massive column onto its back to deliver the final blow.

Liyf

Halfling Cleric

Loviatar's Accolyte

Liyf - a short, feminine halfling - is often mistaken for a child, a perception she has used to great advantage against foes who underestimate her. This priestess of the pain-god Loviatar is not one to be trifled with, wielding death with her Spiritual Weapon, and brining life back with Revivify.

Likes
  • Loviatar
  • Rifling through someone's belongings...for recon
  • Ball bearings
Dislikes
  • When her party keeps dying at inopportune moments
  • Those who disparage Loviatar
  • A boring bar

Signature Moves and Achievements

  • Dropping a bag of ball bearings down a crowded staircase, causing enough havoc to allow the party to escape their pursuers.
  • Literally saving everyone's butt, in every battle, with her healing.
  • Being wildly difficult to hit, while also dealing wild amounts of damage.

Mab

Tielfing Illusion Wizard

The Spooky One

Mab is a powerful, if not fragile, wizard who specializes in the art of illusion, with a strong dash of AOE spells. A charismatic-slash-intimidating loner, she finds solace in books and the pursuit of arcane knowledge. Not used to having friends, she finds herself awkwardly fond of her comrades - though still burdened by the grief and shame of her past.

Likes
  • Books. Libraries. Giant notebooks full of knowledge.
  • Hiding behind her bestie and assigned-tank Siw
  • Scaring the heck out of her enemies
Dislikes
  • Having literally HALF the HP of her friends.
  • Smarmy bards.
  • Not landing a high-level spell she burned her single high-level spell slot for.

Signature Moves and Achievements

  • Once created a convincing enough image of Mictlan to scare half an army. Mictlan gave it a thumbs up.
  • Somehow managing to pass all her rolls, yet getting knocked into death saving throws in each battle and requiring Siw to save her.
  • Casting Firebolt, Agnazzar's Scorcher, or Scorching Ray with an uncouth gesture towards her enemies.

Mikrok

Dragonborn Fighter/War Mage

The Green War Scholar

Mikrok comes from a long line of famous Dragonborn - as scholarly as he is powerful, he seeks arcane wisdom regarding the origins of his people. Tall and muscular, his form can often be intimidating, but his passion for acting draws out the appreciation in his crowds.

Likes
  • Biiiiig weapons
  • A well-written monologue
  • Arcane knowledge - but not in the freaky way Mab pursues it.
Dislikes
  • Rude people
  • Boring plays and hammy actors
  • Intelletual dead ends

Signature Moves and Achievements

Taddy Mason

Mountain Goliath Bard

The Singing Rock

Hailing from the noble Mason family of the Ceacilian mountains, he knew from an early age he was destined for a life of song. Honing his skills as a bard, the unusually charming Goliath sought his fortune in Nettep, where he scored a gig as a featured artist at the hoity-toity hotel the Gilded Lily. Running into the rag-tag group of adventurers called the Thunderbuns while fighting their way out of a dinner party gone bad, he is slowly getting to know his compatriots.

Likes
  • His new best friend, Knot
  • Hot baths
  • Cooking exsquisite meals
Dislikes
  • Lightning and thunder
  • Feet?
  • Technology that's not spell-driven

Signature Moves and Achievements

  • Is ready to bail at the sight of danger, but begrudingly does not leave the team behind.
  • Jamming with anyone and anything. It's how he met his bestie.
  • Once observed his tribe move a mountain, proving that mountains can indeed be moved.
